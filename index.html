Guidelines

  • Each photographer can submit 2 maximum photograph.
  • Photograph must be taken in Kutch.
  • Photographs should be submitted in JPG format with a minimum resolution of 3000 pixels on the larger side.
  • Photos should be sent with details of date, place of photography and name of species.
  • To maintain uniformity, watermark or name of photographer should not be mentioned on photograph.
  • Drawings must be A3 size or larger.
  • Selected photographs will be exhibited by the department.
  • The decision of the department for selection will be final.
  • Photos should be submitted to www.KutchWildlifeWeek.com by Oct 5 9PM.
  • Artwork should be submitted at Office of Deputy Conservator of Forests, Kutch West Division, Arihantnagar Road, Bhuj by 12PM noon on Oct 5.
  • Prize distribution will be for three positions in both photography and artwork.
  • Best 3 photographs and artworks will be awarded by the department on 06-10-2021, which will be selected by the committee of experts formed by the department.
  • All participants will receive a certificate.
  • Prizes will be distributed on Oct 7, at 11AM at the Exhibition.
  • The copyright of all the photographs will be held by the photographer and the Kutch (West) Forest Division. Kutch (West) Forest Division will be free to use the photos wherever and whenever required.
  • The department shall have the right to exhibit and use the submitted photos with due photo courtesy given to the photographer.
